Our Services

Repairs and Restoration

Our book restoration services go beyond basic repairs to fully restore and preserve the beauty and functionality of your books. Whether it's an antique, a family heirloom, or a rare first edition, our experts use traditional techniques combined with modern technology to ensure your book is restored to its former glory.

Book Binding

We offer professional book binding services that combine craftsmanship and quality to create durable, beautiful bindings for your books. Whether you're looking to bind a new manuscript, rebind a cherished book, or create custom bindings, our skilled artisans are here to help.

Learn more
Publishing

We offer comprehensive publishing services designed to bring your literary vision to life. Whether you're an aspiring author, a historian, or a business professional, our team provides the expertise and support needed to transform your manuscript into a polished, published work.

Learn more

Thesis / Dissertation

We specialize in printing and binding theses and dissertations to meet the specific requirements of South African institutions. We understand the importance of presenting your hard work in a professional and durable format, and our services are tailored to ensure your academic achievements are showcased to their fullest potential.

Custom Books

Ah, the joy of something made to your specifications! Sure, there’s a place for the mass produced. However, when you decide on a custom bound book, you choose the style, materials, colours and typefaces. We encourages your input for the binding of your journal, sketchbook, recipe book, thesis, guest book, wedding book, family history, notes for world domination plans or children’s drawings. Want a book to look like a melted ice-cream, or box of cigars? Done…
